Question 1:
In an e-commerce platform, orders are placed throughout the day with order amounts recorded. The analytics team wants to find the difference between the average of the top 3 highest orders and the average of the bottom 3 lowest orders to measure the spending gap. If total orders are less than 6, print "Insufficient Data".

Input Description: 
You are given a number 'n'. Next line contains n space separated integers.
Output Description: 
Print the spending gap (rounded to 2 decimal places).

Sample Input:
7
500 1200 340 890 2100 75 650
Sample Output:
1391.67
